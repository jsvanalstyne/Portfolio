About

I graduated from the University of North Carolina in Chapel Hill where I studied Chemisty and Clinical Laboratory Science. Over the past 7 years, I have created a successful career in a major reference laboratory.

I have always been interested in programming and have decided to pursue my passion! I am currently enrolled in UNC's coding bootcamp with a graduation date of February 2020. Utilizing my previous experince in leadership, teamwork, and critical thinking, I have gained a vast amount experience and knowledge in order to become proficient in JavaScript, Express.JS, Node, Sequelize, GitHub, CSS and more!

In my spare time, I participate in the "Women Who Code" intiative.I frequently engage in "MeetUps" to learn from more experienced developers and to stay up-to-date on trends.

Besides programming, I love spending time with my family and friends. I enjoy traveling and learning.
