About

I graduated from the University of North Carolina in Chapel Hill where I studied Chemisty and Clinical Laboratory Science. Over the past 7 years, I have created a successful career in a major reference laboratory.

I decided to seek a career in web development and computer programming to further my passion for learning and engaging in an ever-expanding field. I am currently enrolled in UNC's coding bootcamp with a graduation date of March 2020. Utilizing my previous expeerince in leadership, teamwork, and critical thinking, I have gained a vast amount experience and knowledge using JavaScript, Node, Git and more.

In my spare time, I participate in the "Women Who Code" intiative.I frequently engage in "MeetUps" to learn from more experienced developers and to stay up-to-date in this evolving field.

Besides programming, I love spending time with my family and friends. I enjoy traveling and learning about other cultures.
