About

I have always been interested in programming and have decided to pursue my passion! I am currently enrolled in UNC's coding bootcamp with a graduation date of February 2020. Utilizing my previous experince in leadership, teamwork, and critical thinking, I have gained a vast amount experience and knowledge in order to become proficient in JavaScript, Express.JS, Node, Sequelize, GitHub, CSS and more!
